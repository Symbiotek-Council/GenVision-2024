    • GENVISION 2024

    GenVision– the signature annual event of Symbiotek Council, is aimed at providing a platform for exploring innovative and creative ideas to guide you towards a great career in Biosciences & Bioengineering. Keeping up with the tradition of 25 successful years, we present to you GenVision 2024. Bringing together high-quality researchers with cutting-edge expertise in basic and applied bio-related area, the event provides a platform for upcoming talents and empowers students with a scientific and technical temperament which provides them leadership for emerging needs of the society.

    EVENTS

    Bio- टक्कर

    A quizzing extravaganza that challenges the abilities of bio-enthusiasts on and off the field. The event aims to inspire the participants in their quest for knowledge.

    Lab Visit

    Embark on an enlightening journey into the heart of scientific innovation at Genvision's Lab Visit event. Witness cutting-edge research and sophisticated facilities, offering a glimpse into the exciting world of bio innovation and bioengineering.

    BioCanvas - Bridging Discoveries, Showcasing Life

    Explore the intricate beauty of life through Biocanvas, a captivating poster presentation event showcasing the artistry of biological sciences. Immerse yourself in the visually stunning world of scientific discovery andinnovation."

    Treasure Hunt - Crack the Code, Find the Gold!

    Embark on a thrilling adventure, unraveling mysteries and chasing clues. Dive deep into an immersive treasure hunt, where excitement awaits at every turn. Unleash your inner explorer and race against time to claim the ultimate prize!

    Debate - Battle of Ideas

    Our Debate Competition aims to bring together passionate individuals who enjoy engaging in thought-provoking discussions and showcasing their eloquence. This event provides a platform for participants to express their opinions, enhance their public speaking skills, and connect with individuals.

    Event X

    Good with biology and also fond of fun classic games like bingo and crosswords? Well then, Event X is here for you!!! A combination of three rounds filled with enigmatic biology puzzles awaits your wit.
